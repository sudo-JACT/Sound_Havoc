About BlackMetal Plugins

BlackMetal Plugins is dedicated to bringing you the darkest and most powerful LV2 plugins for your music production needs.

Our plugins are crafted with the intensity of black metal music, providing you with the tools to create music that will crush your listeners with its raw energy.
